This project analyzes real retail sales data from a superstore to identify revenue trends and business insights. The dataset is from Kaggle called Superstore Sales Dataset. it contains sales transtions with columns like Region, Category, Segment, Ship Mode, and Sales.

Questions:
"We need to understand our regional performance — can you tell me which region is bringing in the most revenue?"
"Leadership is meeting next week and wants to know where our strongest product categories are — can you pull that data?"
"Marketing is planning their next campaign and needs to know which customer segment is spending the most with us — can you look into that?"
"Operations is asking about our shipping — can you find out which shipping method our customers are using the most?"

Key Findings
1. The West region generated the highest revenue - $710,219
2. Technology was the top performing category
3. The consumer segment had spent the most, which was $1,148,060
4. Standard class was the most used shipping method with 5,859 orders

5. Tools Used:
6. SQL, DB Browser for SQLite

Notes:
The dataset is from Kaggle, and AI was periodically used to check my approach. All queries were done myself
